Our Story

Perched on the breathtaking cliffs of Santorini, our restaurant brings the vibrant spirit of Greece to life right here in Alexandria. Inspired by the iconic white-washed buildings and azure waters of the Aegean Sea, Santorini Greek Restaurant was founded by a family of Greek immigrants who dreamed of sharing the authentic flavors of their homeland.

From the sun-kissed olive groves to the fresh catch of the day, every dish is crafted with traditional recipes passed down through generations. Imagine savoring moussaka while gazing at the ocean breeze, or enjoying a quick souvlaki bite under the stars. Our 24-hour doors welcome everyone—from families with kids to lively groups—creating a cosy, casual haven for Greek culinary adventures.

Over the years, we've become a beloved spot for those craving the warmth of Greek hospitality. Whether it's a late-night tzatziki dip or a festive gathering, Santorini Greek Restaurant is where memories are made. Join us and let the flavors of the islands transport you—your Greek feast awaits!
